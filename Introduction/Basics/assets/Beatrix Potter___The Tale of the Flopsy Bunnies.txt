The Tale of the Flopsy Bunnies by Beatrix Potter

"The Tale of the Flopsy Bunnies" is a children's book by Beatrix Potter. It follows the adventures of six young bunnies who are the children of Peter Rabbit and his wife, Flopsy. The bunnies sneak into a garden to gather vegetables but are captured by Mr. McGregor. With the help of their parents, they manage to escape and enjoy a delicious meal of bread, milk, and blackberries.
